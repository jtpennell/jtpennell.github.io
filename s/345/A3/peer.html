Explosive back exercise

When training your back and would like to do explosive back exercises, there are a number of options on how to do so. One method is by doing pullups, where you basically grab a bar and use your upper body strength to pull yourself up. If it is too easy for you, you can add some resistance by adding weights to a belt. This, as a result with strengthen your back and may with posture.
